    Great movie. Acting is great, cinematography is great and the soundtrack is great. It's very realistic, and I know that because even though I have not been to space people actually thought it was filmed in space. Yes, that's pretty stupid, but if it can convince you of something that ridiculous it must be spot on. Does not even compare to a masterpiece like Interstellar, and is still probably worse than The Martian, but overall in my opinion the three of them are the best space movies out there. One complaint I have is that the reason Ryan is named Ryan and is a girl is that "her dad wanted a boy". That's kind of messed up, but I really have no idea why they added it, it's never mentioned again and adds nothing. I guess that's not really a negative but it's not a positive, they couldn't have just given her a girl name? If you love space (like me) then this movie is for you, and if you don't like space this movie will make you like space (even though one of Sandra's lines is literally "I hate space").
